St. Albert the Great | S.Y 2024 - 2025

Grade 9 - St. Albert the Great was a section of excellence, excelling in academics, sports, and character. Students showcased intelligence, teamwork, and dedication, consistently ranking among the top performers. Beyond the classroom, they were fierce yet fair competitors in sports, displaying both skill and sportsmanship. Their discipline and unity were evident in their confidence and pride as they represented their section. Our Beloved Teachers

Miss Rona (MAPEH)

MAPEH has been a journey of creativity, expression, and movement, with retrospection of past cultures. Through music, arts, PE, and health, we have learned to appreciate our talents, stay active, and embrace a well-rounded lifestyle. We miss the exciting activities and the way you encouraged us to shine in different forms of expression.

Sir Ron (ICT)

ICT has given us useful knowledge when handling computers, both internally and externally. From the meaning of ICT to how to handle computers, your lessons have taught us practical knowledge that we will carry into the future. We miss your recitations and how you guided us with patience and expertise.

Sir Yobs (Science)

Science has been an exciting journey of discovery and curiosity, in accordance with practicality and boundaries. You have inspired us to explore the wonders of the universe, from the smallest particles to the vast cosmos. We miss the engaging experiments and the way you made learning both fun and meaningful.

Sir DJ (CF)

CF has truly shaped our values and deepened our faith, giving us a better look towards religious figures. Your guidance has taught us kindness, compassion, and integrity, helping us grow not just academically but spiritually. We miss the meaningful discussions and the wisdom you share with us.

Miss Nina (Araling Panlipunan)

Araling Panlipunan has helped us understand how the economy works and establish a simple basis on economics. Your lessons have given us a sense of responsibility when looking into the economy. We miss the engaging discussions and the way you explain Mathematics throughout our lessons.

Sir Mapa (Math)

Math has challenged and strengthened our problem-solving skills by making it difficult but rewarding in a way. Your patience and clear explanations have helped us build confidence in numbers. We miss the step-by-step guidance that made complex equations feel achievable and the never-ending book/daily learning activities.

Ginang Lyka (Filipino)

Filipino has deepened our love for our language and culture, with our national hero, Jose Rizal, as the pillar. Through literature and discussion, you have shown us the beauty and power of our words. We miss the inspiring lessons that remind us of the richness of our heritage.
